About Me

For the last decade, Tristan has owned and operated Stickerslug Incorporated. Before becoming a business owner, Tristan went to school for business administration at the University of Oregon. He have spent the better part of a decade learning the ins and outs of business ownership. Tristan Emmerson resides in Portland, Oregon with his wife and 2 young boys. Tristan has been with his wife since they were college sweet-hearts in 2006. His two young boys, Cohen & Avery are 4 and 2, respectively. It is quite possible, his wife is a superhero.
